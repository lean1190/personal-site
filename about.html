About Hi, I'm Lean

Your success is my purpose

I love putting inspirational sparks in someone's eyes.
Coaching you to succeed is my way of fulfilling my purpose.

I'm also a software engineer who enjoys mentoring people and crafting solid solutions to difficult problems.

When we work together, you can expect from me someone who values relationships, helps you explore new paths, and challenges you.

I believe in a world where we all have less stress and more fulfillment in our lives, and I believe communication is the starting point to achieve this.
Any change in our lives requires communicating with ourselves and others to make it happen. This is where communication connects to my second passion, ideation.
I enjoy breaking the rules of the status quo, I enjoy finding new ways of doing things and lighting a spark in someone's eyes.

My passion for communication comes from my own need to land a message effectively. I keep learning about the topic through books, speakers, and podcasts, starting from “Difficult Conversations” by Bruce Patton, Douglas Stone, and Sheila Heen, which I recommend to everyone. Trying to understand how people communicate sparked my curiosity and joy. Since then, I've been training myself to get better at delivering my message and moving towards a more assertive communication style. My interest in communication helped me support my ideation frenzy. I've always questioned how things are done, not because I wanna be annoying, but because I believe there's always room for improvement. Change is not easy, it is usually slow and requires a lot of energy, which is why communication goes a long way here. I usually start with a simple method that I borrowed from Ricardo Semler, the “3 whys”. The “3 whys” is asking “why” 3 times to see if the argument resists the pressure. If it does and the argument is solid, there's probably little need for change given the current circumstances. If the argument doesn't hold, that's when my brain starts to spark.
