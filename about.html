How I Got Here

I believe in a world where we all have less stress and more fulfillment in our lives, and I believe communication is the starting point to achieve this. Any change in our lives requires communicating with ourselves and others to make it happen. This is where communication connects to my second passion, ideation.

I enjoy breaking the rules of the status quo, I enjoy finding new ways of doing things and lighting a spark in someone's eyes.
My passion for communication comes from my own need to land a message as I hoped. I was born with a short fuse, whenever someone would not get what I said I would get nuts. We can all agree that this is not ideal, and the more experience and influence I had, the more I needed to land my message respectfully and effectively. So I took a step back and reflected on my communication style, I decided I needed to do something different. My interest in communication helps me support my ideation frenzy. I've always questioned how things are done because I believe there's always room for improvement.
